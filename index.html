About the project

What is gr-sw-maker?

A framework where AI handles everything — specs, design, implementation, testing, and delivery — from a single concept description. You write what you want to build, and receive the finished product.

01
Write a concept
What you want to build, and why. That's all you need to provide.
02
AI runs all 8 phases
Specs → design → implementation → testing → delivery. Quality gates at every step.
03
Receive the deliverable
Code, tests, docs, and deployment — all delivered together.

Domain transfer

We'll hand this domain
to the right hands.

happy-soft-maker.org will be transferred — free of charge — to an organization or individual who can drive gr-sw-maker forward as an enterprise-level OSS project.

  • 🌍 Committed to growing it openly as OSS and contributing to the broader community
  • 🏢 Has the organizational resources and sustained will to push the project forward at scale
  • 💡 Genuinely aligned with the vision: make it easy for everyone to build software that makes people happy

このプロジェクトについて

gr-sw-maker とは

コンセプトを書くだけで、AIが仕様・設計・実装・テスト・デリバリーまでを自動で進めるフレームワークです。 あなたがやることは「何を作りたいか」を伝え、成果物を受け取るだけ。

01
コンセプトを書く
何を作りたいか、なぜ必要か。それだけでいい。
02
AIが全8フェーズを進める
仕様→設計→実装→テスト→デリバリー。各フェーズに品質ゲートあり。
03
成果物を受け取る
コード・テスト・ドキュメント・デプロイまでセットで届く。
